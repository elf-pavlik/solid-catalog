Solid Resources Catalog

This catalog is a work in progress and not all data has been recently updated. Please help by updataing records for yourself, your organization, and your projects.

There are three main ways to navigate the catalog:

  • Use the list of resource types at the left of the screenb
  • Use the full text searchbar at the top of the screen
  • Use the keyword index at the top of the screen
  • Navigate to a record and jump to related records

If you do not find a record for yourself or your projects, use the *new record* button at the top of the screen to create one.

To read more about the catalog and other viewers for it, see the gitihub repository and/or catch me (@jeff-zucker) on matrix.
